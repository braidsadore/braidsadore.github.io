Welcome to Braids Adore by Bisi Appointment Booking

To book an appointment, please follow these steps:

  1. Select a hairstyle from the list provided below, along with the corresponding price.
  2. Choose a date and time that works for you from the available hourly slots provided on the calendar.
  3. A $30 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. You can pay the deposit through the website using Zelle or Cash App to the phone number (667) 324-9721 or to areadebisi@gmail.com.
  4. Arrive for your appointment with clean, washed and blow-dried hair.
  5. Please note that the remaining balance of your hair service should be paid in cash or Zelle.

Instructions:

  • Your hair must be at least 4 inches long.
  • Do not apply a fresh relaxer before your appointment.
  • Do not use any oil in your hair on the day of your appointment.
  • If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please do so at least 72 hours before your scheduled appointment to use your deposit for a future appointment. If you cancel less than 72 hours before your appointment, you will need to pay a new deposit to book again.
  • Please inform us of any known allergies to braiding hair and/or hair products.
